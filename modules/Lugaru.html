Module Lugaru

Lugaru is the toplevel framework namespace.

It includes most related resources off of it.

Functions

init (width, height)
One-time initialization of Lugaru.
This is required for non-mobile environments.

Parameters:

  • width: width of window, in pixels
  • height: width of window, in pixels

Usage:

    local L = require 'lugaru'
 L.init(640, 480)
