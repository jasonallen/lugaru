Module Scene

Scenes are a way of organizing objects.

They represent distinct parts of the game. These can be different screens or levels, or overlays. Scenes are rendered by their priority. Scenes are setup and torn down by responding to their 'added' and 'removed' callbacks.

Usage:

     local MyScene = class(Scene)
 function MyScene:added(sceneMgr)
 sceneMgr:add(SpaceShipEntity())
 end

Functions

Scene:start () Will make a scene visible and activate all the entities contained within.
Scene:stop () Hides and removes a scene, deactivating all the entities contained.
